Application Log Collection and Storage

Overview

The Cloud Platform automatically collects and stores your application logs, and lets you access them via an AWS-Hosted Kibana dashboard.

Logs are collected via Fluentbit and Fluentd, and stored in an AWS-Hosted ElasticSearch Cluster.

Log Collection

Fluentbit/Fluentd is a cluster-wide log collection service.

The Fluentbit and Fluentd applications are configured with cluster-wide read permissions. As long as your application logs to stdout, your logs will be collected.

Log Storage

The logs are shipped to an AWS-Hosted ElasticSearch cluster. All application logs are retained for 30 days.
